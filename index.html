I am a postdoctoral fellow at Yau Center , Tsinghua University. My mentor is Bin Xu. Before, I was a PhD student at Aix-Marseille University under the supervision of Volker Heiermann.

Research

My research interests lie in the interface of representation theory and number theory. I have been interested in representations of reductive groups over p-adic fields, and distinction problems.
